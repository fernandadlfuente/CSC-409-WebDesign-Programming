Punta Cana Travel Plans!

First time in Punta Cana? Get ready for an unforgettable trip! Located on the eastern coast of the Dominican Republic, this tropical paradise is known for its beautiful beaches, clear blue waters and tropical vibes. Follow along to discover some fun excursions, tasty local eats, and must-see spots that capture the island's beauty and culture with friends or family. Whether you're looking for adventure or relaxation, this itinerary has something for everyone and plenty of moments that'll make your instagram jealous!

Scape Park at Cap Cana

The first place I would recommend going is called Scape Park at Cap Cana. Its for all ages! Whether you seek adventure, relaxation or cultural immersion there's something for everyone! From ziplining through lush canopies to exploring mysterious caves and cenotes. For families with kids, miniscape is an area specifically designed for them offering obstacle courses, diving into crystalline waters and waterfalls.

Saona Island

The second place I would recommend going is called Saona Island. Its a separate Island where you hop on a speed boat for them to take you to the Island. They drive you through the ocean with the bluest and clearest water. The first stop the boat takes you is what they called "a natural swimming pool" in the middle of the Caribbean where everyone just enjoys their time especially with a floating bar. From there, they take you to the actual island where they have lunch and loungers.

Macao Beach

The third place I would recommend going is called Macao beach. You can go horseback riding and swim with horses. You can also go on ATVs where they'll take you through farmlands and swimming holes in caves.

Coco Bongo

The fourth place I would recommend going is more of a night life activity, its called Coco Bongo. It is a large famous nightlife venue which has dance performances, a mix of a lot of music, open bars and interactive shows. The experience lasts for about 3 hours and is followed by a large open party that continues until the next morning.
